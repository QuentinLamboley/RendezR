# Code of Conduct

RendezR LAN is intended for respectful, consensual and limited local conversations among people who have independently opted in.

Do not harass, threaten, discriminate, sexualize, solicit, impersonate, dox, stalk, distribute unwanted content, share personal details, send credentials, or attempt to identify another peer. Leave the conversation or block a peer when needed.

Use the software only on networks you are authorised to use. Do not exploit or broaden its local discovery functionality.

Project maintainers may remove contributions or restrict project participation for conduct inconsistent with these expectations.
